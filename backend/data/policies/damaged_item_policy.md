# Damaged Item Policy

## Eligibility

Customers may report an item as damaged when the product arrives broken, defective, incomplete, or visibly affected during delivery.

A damaged-item request should normally be submitted within 7 days of delivery.

## Required Information

The customer should provide:

- The order identifier.
- A description of the damage.
- Whether the external packaging was damaged.
- Photographic evidence when available.
- Information about any missing parts or accessories.

## Automatic Review Conditions

The request may proceed through the automated review workflow when:

- The order status is `delivered`.
- Delivery occurred within the previous 7 days.
- The customer identity matches the order record.
- The product is not listed as a restricted item.
- The information provided is complete and consistent.

## Replacement or Refund

A replacement may be recommended when the same product is available and the customer prefers a replacement.

A refund may be recommended when:

- A replacement is unavailable.
- The product is unusable.
- Essential parts are missing.
- The customer has experienced repeated delivery damage.

## Human Review

The request must be escalated to a human support agent when:

- The customer identity cannot be verified.
- The damage was reported more than 7 days after delivery.
- The information or evidence is incomplete.
- The product is high-value, personalised, or restricted.
- The customer reports possible safety risks.
- The order contains multiple damaged items.

## Limitations

The AI support agent may recommend a replacement, refund, or human review.

The AI support agent must not issue a refund, create a replacement shipment, or determine whether photographic evidence is authentic.

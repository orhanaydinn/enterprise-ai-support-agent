# Cancellation Policy

## Eligibility

Customers may request an order cancellation before the order has been shipped.

Orders with the status `processing` may be eligible for automatic cancellation.

Orders with the status `shipped`, `delivered`, `cancelled`, or `refunded` cannot be cancelled automatically.

## Processing Orders

A processing order may be cancelled when:

- Payment has been confirmed.
- The order has not been assigned to a delivery carrier.
- Warehouse fulfilment has not been completed.
- No restricted or personalised item is included in the order.

## Shipped Orders

An order that has already been shipped cannot be cancelled.

The customer should wait for delivery and then follow the applicable return or refund policy.

## Personalised and Restricted Items

Personalised products cannot normally be cancelled after production has started.

Restricted items, including hygiene-sensitive products, may require human review before cancellation.

## Human Review

The request must be escalated to a human support agent when:

- The fulfilment status cannot be verified.
- A personalised item is included in the order.
- Warehouse processing has already started.
- The customer provides conflicting order information.
- The order contains multiple items with different fulfilment statuses.

## Cancellation Execution

The AI support agent may determine whether an order appears eligible for cancellation.

The AI support agent must not cancel an order or issue a payment reversal directly.

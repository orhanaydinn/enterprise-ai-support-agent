# Shipping Policy

## Processing Times

Orders are normally processed within 1 to 2 business days after payment confirmation.

Orders placed during weekends or public holidays may be processed on the next business day.

## Shipping Status

An order with the status `processing` has not yet been handed to the delivery carrier.

An order with the status `shipped` has been handed to the delivery carrier and is in transit.

An order with the status `delivered` has been marked as delivered by the carrier.

## Tracking Information

Tracking information should normally become available within 24 hours after an order is marked as `shipped`.

A customer should be advised to wait up to 24 hours when an order has recently changed to `shipped` but tracking information is not yet visible.

## Delivery Delays

Standard delivery normally takes 3 to 5 business days after shipment.

A delivery should be considered delayed when:

- The estimated delivery date has passed.
- No tracking update has been recorded for more than 3 business days.
- The carrier reports an operational delay.

## Human Review

The request must be escalated to a human support agent when:

- The tracking number is missing after 24 hours.
- The package has not moved for more than 3 business days.
- The carrier marks the package as lost.
- The customer reports that a delivered package was not received.

## Limitations

The AI support agent may explain shipping status and recommend next steps, but it must not contact the carrier or issue compensation directly.

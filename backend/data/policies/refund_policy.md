# Refund Policy

## Eligibility

Customers may request a refund within 14 days of delivery.

The order must have a status of `delivered`.

Orders that are still processing, shipped, cancelled, or already refunded are not eligible for an automatic refund.

## Human Review

The request must be escalated to a human support agent when:

- The delivery date cannot be verified.
- The order was delivered more than 14 days ago.
- The customer identity does not match the order record.
- The request contains unusual or conflicting information.

## Refund Execution

The AI support agent may recommend a refund decision, but it must not execute a financial refund directly.
